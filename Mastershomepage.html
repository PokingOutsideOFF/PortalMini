After completing your undergraduate you have a plethora of options available however the most common one’s are:

  1. Master's Degree from foreign universities(MS):

    To pursue a Masters degree from USA you need to give GRE i.e. GRADUATE RECORD EXAMINATION along with TOEFL. Based on your scores you can apply to various universities ,for European universities along with TOEFL you need to give IELTS exam. This is generally preffered by students who wish to obtain a specialization in their undergraduate domain.

  2. Master's in Business Administration(MBA):

    One of the most chosen branch of masters is MBA. If you have interest in starting your own startup or have a cling towards marketing, finance etc. then MBA is meant for you. To pursue MBA from IIM which are the top ranked Business schools in India you need to give the CAT exam. If you wish to pursue MBA from any other university in the world you need to appear for GMAT

  3. Master's of Technology(MTech):

    If you are an engineering undergraduate you can give GATE exam and secure a seat in one of the IIT’S and complete your MTech in engineering.
